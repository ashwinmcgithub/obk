Our Story

Born from a Love for Biryani

OBK — Oru Biriyani Kadha — is not just a restaurant, it's a story. Nestled in the heart of Old Mink Food Street, Thodupuzha, OBK was born from a simple dream: to serve the most flavourful, authentic biryani Kerala has ever tasted.

Every grain of rice, every whole spice, every slow-cooked piece of meat tells the story of a craft perfected with passion. We use traditional Kerala recipes, fresh local ingredients, and age-old dum cooking techniques to bring you a biryani that stays in your memory long after the last bite.
